EXPLORE OUR GYM

Café Revive

An onsite coffee shop. Grab a coffee and a snack before or after your workout. A selection of healthy food and drinks are available. Be social!

Multimediaroom
Cork’s Sporting Heros Multimedia Room: A multimedia room open to gym members only. Contains video, audio and memorabilia of some of Cork’s most famous sports stars including Roy Keane, Denis Irwin, Sonia O’Sullivan, Christy Ring and Ronan O’Gara.

Swimming Pool

Our 18-metre pool is kept at a constant, comfortable temperature with a dedicated swim lane. After your swim, why not relax in our Jacuzzi, sauna, steam room or spa pool.
